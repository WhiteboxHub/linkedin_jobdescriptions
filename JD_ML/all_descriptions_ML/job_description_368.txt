About the Role
We are seeking a detail-oriented and skilled Data Analyst with a background in the telecommunications industry. The ideal candidate will have experience analyzing complex datasets, identifying trends, and making data-driven decisions to enhance business operations and strategies. This role requires a deep understanding of telecom data, strong analytical skills, and the ability to work in a hybrid environment.

Key Responsibilities:
- Analyze large and complex telecom datasets to identify trends, patterns, and insights that can drive business decisions.
- Develop and maintain data models, reports, and dashboards to support various business functions.
- 3+ years of experience as a Data Analyst, preferably within the telecommunications industry.
- Proficiency in data analysis tools such as SQL, Python, R, or similar.
- Experience with data visualization tools like Tableau, Power BI, or equivalent.
- Strong knowledge of telecom datasets, including customer data, network data, and billing data.
- Excellent problem-solving skills and attention to detail.
- Strong communication skills with the ability to convey complex data insights to non-technical stakeholders.
- Experience working in a hybrid work environment, with the ability to collaborate effectively both remotely and in-person.
