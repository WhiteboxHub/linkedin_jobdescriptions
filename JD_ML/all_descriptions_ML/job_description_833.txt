About the job
Company Description
 
We provide in person and virtual weight loss and personal training and functional medicine services. 

 Role Description
 
This is a full-time on-site role as a Weight Loss Coach/Personal Trainer at Alpha Weight Loss in Cary, NC. As a Weight Loss Coach/Personal Trainer, you will be responsible for guiding and supporting clients in their weight loss journey, creating personalized fitness and nutrition plans, conducting workout sessions, and providing motivation and accountability to help clients achieve their goals.

 Qualifications
 
Certification in Personal Training or Fitness Coaching
Strong knowledge of nutrition, weight loss principles, and fitness techniques
Excellent communication and interpersonal skills
Experience in developing personalized training programs
Ability to motivate and inspire clients
Knowledge of health and safety regulations in fitness settings
Previous experience working with clients on weight loss goals
