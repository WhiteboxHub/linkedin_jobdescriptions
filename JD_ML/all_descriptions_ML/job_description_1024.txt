About the job
As a Senior Lead Data Engineer, you will be responsible for leading complex project work as a part of a larger cross-functional team in support of Supply Chain. Sr. Lead Data Engineers typically design, implement, maintain, and optimize necessary analytics infrastructure on technically complex projects, focusing on developing solutions to data modeling and/or analytics technology needs. You must have a general understanding of Supply Chain operations and be able to quickly develop mastery of the needed subject area across Supply Chain.

Our Senior Lead Data Engineers work with wide latitude for action or decisions on projects that are more complex and cross-functional in nature. They act as a subject-matter-expert and resource on data and/or analytics technology, and they may represent Supply Chain Data Analytics (SCDA) in external conversations. They also assist with the selection or oversight of outside vendor partners. They are expected to influence the project execution of IT and analyst teams. Successful candidates for this role must have strong analytical and data modeling skills, a deep understanding of database technology and data use within analytic platforms, and they must have strong programming skills with SQL and/or Python.

We expect our Senior Lead Data Engineers to have demonstrated fluency and competency in both the technology language of IT and the analyst language of Supply Chain analysts. They have experience developing and implementing data models for analytic use and a moderate level of experience with cloud database architecture. They will need to be fast learners with a keen eye for detail, systems thinking, and process design. They must be team players who work steadfastly to create impactful change.

Our Flexible Future model offers a healthy mix of working in person and remotely, strengthening key elements of the Chick-fil-A culture by fostering collaboration and community.

Responsibilities

The person who fills this role will be expected to do the following as a part of their regular work responsibilities:

 Exhibit wide latitude in aligning Supply Chain stakeholders and IT to translate business logic into scalable data and analytic solutions on complex projects 
 Develop ETL pipelines for Supply Chain consumption, establishing patterns for repeatable, scalable ETL work that others can leverage in their work 
 Develop tool, data, and analytic best practices for SCDA and advise on their integration into operational practices 
 Design specific tool implementations, understanding Databricks, DBT performance, and cloud compute performance requirements and expectations 
 Help design patterns for efficient use of cloud compute resources 
 Own and be accountable for data model and code quality and any relevant documentation 
 Implement data governance and master data management principles across specific project execution 
 Act as a subject-matter-expert and resource on data and/or analytic technology issues for the larger SCDA community 
 Drive complex projects to success and enables others to own the impact 
 Manage any needed maintenance and quality control on data pipelines or data products they have built which are actively in use
