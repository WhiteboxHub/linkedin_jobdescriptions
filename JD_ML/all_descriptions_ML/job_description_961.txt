Analyze business processes to identify opportunities for automation using UiPath.
Collaborate with stakeholders to gather and document requirements for automation projects.
Develop detailed process documentation, including process maps, business requirements, and use cases.
Work closely with UiPath developers to translate business requirements into technical solutions.
Conduct feasibility studies and impact analyses for potential automation projects.
Assist in the design, testing, and deployment of UiPath automation solutions.
Provide training and support to end-users and stakeholders on UiPath solutions.
Continuously monitor and evaluate the effectiveness of implemented automation solutions.
Stay updated with the latest UiPath features, tools, and best practices
