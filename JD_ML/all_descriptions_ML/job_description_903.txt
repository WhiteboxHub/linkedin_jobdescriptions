4. Leverage experience with the Intelligence Community (IC), including non-DoD IC elements.
5. Specialize in one or more of the following subfields:
• AI architecture
• AI engineering
• AI model development
• AI research
• Data architecture
• Data engineering and/or library sciences
6. Ensure AI assurance, AI security, and AI privacy.
7. Be forward-leaning and recommend necessary improvements to achieve the Customer’s vision.

Required Qualifications:

• Excellent analytical and problem-solving skills.

• Must possess a Top Secret/SCI clearance with Full-scope polygraph.

Desired Skills:
