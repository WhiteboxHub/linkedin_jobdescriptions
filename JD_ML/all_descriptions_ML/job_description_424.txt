About the job
Citi’s US in-house creative agency is looking for a data analyst to review performance data and market trends to strengthen its creatives. Our ideal candidate can consolidate complex data and present the findings in an easily understandable way, breaking down deep insights that designers and copywriters can apply to creative development. This analyst will also be responsible for tracking creative results and presenting the discoveries to senior leaders.

 

 

Key Responsibilities:

Monitor and forecast trends to integrate into marketing creatives 
Work closely with internal clients to collect performance results on in-house agency produced deliverables and report on the findings 
Collect competitor work samples, identify key takeaways, and share with the team 
Develop and implement a system for gathering and analyzing data, establishing baselines 
Uncover customer insights through performance result analysis to advise copy and design and strengthen engagement 
Lead lunch and learns with the in-house team and with marketing partners on marketing best practices 
Present key learnings in a clear way to a senior-level audience 
Think strategically to understand business needs and recommend ways of executing them through creative deliverables 
Distill information from multiple sources and synthesize it into one central, compelling story 
Work with internal clients to understand the strategy behind the key message 
Effectively manage time to track multiple active campaigns, delivering high quality work 

Qualifications:

8-10 years of related work experience (digital agency environment preferred), exceptional reporting and presentation skills 
Excellent written and verbal communication skills 
Proficient in MS Office applications, especially Excel, Word and PowerPoint 
Experience using Tableau software is a plus 
Must be able to report data and metrics in a comprehensive manner 
Must be able to glean customer insights from creative performance data 
Adaptable, Bold, Collaborative, Courageous and Determined and operates with a lot of empathy both in internal interactions and the clients 
Experience presenting to senior leaders 
Expert at reporting on market trends and performance data analysis to strengthen campaigns 
Ability to work with the key business stakeholders, understand the central strategy and work with a team to execute effectively 
Passion for analytical thinking and making complex data clear 
An independent leader that’s a self-starter with strong organizational skills 
Eagerness to work in a fast-paced environment 
Strong sense of urgency to meet or exceed deadline expectations 
Tech savvy; able to quickly adapt to new technology and assist team members in troubleshooting issues 

Education:

Bachelor’s degree in Business, Marketing, Mass Communication/Advertising, or related field or equivalent experience.
