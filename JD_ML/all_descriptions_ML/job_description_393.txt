About T&S
Building a world where people can safely discover, create and connect. The Trust & Safety (T&S) team at TikTok helps ensure that our global online community is safe and empowered to create and enjoy content across all of our applications. We have invested heavily in human and machine-based moderation to remove harmful content quickly and often before it reaches our general community.
It is possible that this role will be exposed to harmful content as part of the core role/as part of project/ in response to escalation requests/by chance.
This may occur in the form of images, video, and text related to every-day life, but it can also include (but is not limited to) bullying; hate speech; child safety; depictions of harm to self and others, and harm to animals.

What will I be doing?
- Design and define operation management-related metrics, and build metric management schemes for global products. These metrics may include, for instance, data related to efficiency & quality performance of global moderation teams.
- Provide data support for business reports, run analysis and collect RCA feedback. Follow up with further management actions.
- Build and maintain data infrastructure, conduct data cleaning, troubleshooting and other data consulting work, manage data access, establish a single source of truth for global operation management.
- Push platform tools' iteration, usage and the configurations' completeness and accuracy, to further achieve data visualization and data accuracy in cooperation with multi-functional departments.
- Identify issue areas through data analysis and independently provide solutions & improvement insights to global management teams.
- Actively invocate resources, and the related projects may involve intensive cross-functional cooperation.
