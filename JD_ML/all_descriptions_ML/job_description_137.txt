About the job
Position Overview: We are seeking a skilled Google Analytics Specialist (Volunteer) to join our team. This role requires 3-5 years of hands-on experience with Google Analytics and a deep understanding of web analytics and data interpretation. The ideal candidate will be responsible for analyzing website data to optimize marketing strategies and improve user experience.

Main Functional Role: The Google Analytics Specialist will be responsible for implementing, managing, and analyzing web analytics solutions to provide actionable insights that drive business growth and improve the customer journey.

Key Interactions:

Collaborate with marketing, product management, and development teams
Work closely with stakeholders to understand business requirements and translate them into technical specifications for web analytics

Main Responsibilities:

Implement and manage Google Analytics tracking across various digital platforms
Analyze website traffic and user behavior to identify trends, insights, and opportunities for optimization
Create custom reports and dashboards to monitor key performance indicators (KPIs)
Conduct A/B testing and conversion rate optimization (CRO) to improve website performance
Provide actionable recommendations based on data analysis to inform marketing strategies and product development
Ensure data accuracy and integrity by performing regular audits and troubleshooting tracking issues

Technical/Experience Requirements:

Bachelor's degree in Marketing, Data Science, Computer Science, or a related field
3-5 years of experience with Google Analytics, including implementation, management, and analysis
Proficiency in Google Tag Manager for managing tracking codes and tags
Experience with data visualization tools such as Looker, Tableau, or similar
Strong understanding of web analytics concepts, including segmentation, funnel analysis, and attribution modeling
Working knowledge of SQL and Python is required and familiarity with HTML, and JavaScript is a plus
Google Analytics certification is preferred
Excellent analytical and problem-solving skills with a keen attention to detail
Strong communication skills to present insights and recommendations to non-technical stakeholders

Mindset and Character:

Analytical thinker with a data-driven approach to decision-making
Proactive and self-motivated with a strong sense of ownership and accountability
Ability to work collaboratively in a team environment
Detail-oriented with a commitment to maintaining data integrity and accuracy
