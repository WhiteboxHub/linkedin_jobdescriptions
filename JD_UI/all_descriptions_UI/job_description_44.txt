About the job

Location: Remote

We are currently seeking candidates who meet the following qualifications:

Key Responsibilities

Collaborate with product managers, designers, and developers to understand project requirements and user needs.
Design intuitive and visually appealing user interfaces for web and mobile applications.
Develop wireframes, mockups, and prototypes to communicate design concepts and interactions.
Implement responsive and accessible UI designs using HTML, CSS, and JavaScript frameworks.

Requirements

Bachelor's degree in Computer Science, Graphic Design, or related field.
Proven experience as a UI/UX Developer, Frontend Developer, or similar role.
Proficiency in HTML5, CSS3, JavaScript, and popular frontend frameworks (e.g., React, Angular, Vue).
Experience with design tools such as Adobe XD, Sketch, or Figma.
Strong understanding of user-centered design principles and usability best practices.
Familiarity with responsive design techniques and mobile-first development.
Excellent problem-solving and analytical skills.
Effective communication and collaboration skills in a remote work environment.
