Develop and implement customer-facing website features, adhering to UX best practices and business goals.

Strong grasp of UX/UI principles and best practices.
Proficient in using analytics tools such as GA4, Hotjar, and Content Square.
Excellent communication skills, both written and verbal, capable of explaining technical concepts to non-technical audiences.

Demonstrated understanding of user needs and a commitment to improving their digital experiences.
