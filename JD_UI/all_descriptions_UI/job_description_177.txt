About the job
Responsibilities

A client with Kforce is seeking a Software Engineer, React Native to join their team remotely with some travel required for this position. The React Native Engineer plays a critical role in delivering innovation and growth for the Banking Solutions business by significantly contributing to the technical direction and execution of our mobile retail banking applications. Responsibilities:

 Deliver mobile applications within the Digital domain, ensuring alignment with business goals, security standards, and regulatory requirements
 Implement scalable, robust, and maintainable mobile application code that supports a wide range of financial services and functionalities
 Collaborate closely with product managers, UX/UI designers, backend developers, QA and other stakeholders to understand business requirements and translate them into technical solutions
 Support transition of Angular Ionic codebase to React Native
 Lead by example: implement large and complex features quickly and with excellent code quality and ensure that critical customer-impacting issues are remediated with urgency
 Conduct code reviews and provide technical guidance to developers to ensure adherence to architectural principles, coding standards, and best practices
 Provide feedback, as needed, on proposed third-party tools, frameworks, and APIs, considering factors such as security, performance, and scalability
 Stay up to date with industry trends, emerging technologies, and regulatory changes in the FinTech sector, and incorporate changes into the mobile application as needed

Requirements

 Bachelor's degree in Computer Engineering; Preferred degree in Engineering, Computer Science, or similar relevant field
 8-10 years of experience
 Extensive experience in developing mobile applications for iOS and Android platforms, particularly within the financial technology (FinTech) domain
 Strong proficiency in React Native, Angular and Ionic Capacitor
 Deep understanding of mobile application architecture patterns (e.g., MVC, MVVM, Clean Architecture)
 Experience in transitioning a codebase from Angular Ionic to React Native would be a huge positive
 Knowledge of secure coding practices, encryption algorithms, and data protection mechanisms relevant to handling sensitive financial data in mobile applications
 Scale the application to handle a large user base, considering various devices and network conditions
 Familiarity with backend technologies, APIs, and microservices architecture, enabling seamless integration between apps and backend systems
 Experience in performance optimization, scalability, and troubleshooting of mobile applications to ensure optimal user experience, reliability and responsiveness
 Understanding of financial services industry regulations, compliance requirements (e.g., GDPR, PCI DSS), and security standards related to mobile app development
 Experience building a white label mobile application that is branded and released for multiple clients
 Experience collaborating with India-based development teams
 Deep understanding and commitment to software engineering principles and processes (e.g., Lean, Agile, DevOps) and continuous improvement through measurement
