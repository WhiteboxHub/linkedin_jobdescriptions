About the job
Job Title: UI/UX Developer (US Citizen Only)

Location: Remote

We are currently seeking candidates who meet the following qualifications

Responsibilities

Collaborate with product managers, designers, and developers to define and implement innovative UI/UX solutions for product direction, visuals, and experience.
Create wireframes, storyboards, user flows, process flows, and site maps to effectively communicate interaction and design ideas.
Develop high-quality front-end code using HTML, CSS, JavaScript, and related web technologies.
Ensure the technical feasibility of UI/UX designs.
Optimize application for maximum speed and scalability.
Conduct user research and evaluate user feedback to iterate on the design.
Establish and promote design guidelines, best practices, and standards.
Stay up-to-date with the latest UI/UX trends, techniques, and technologies.
Test for usability and fix any UX issues.

Qualifications

Bachelor’s degree in Computer Science, Design, or a related field.
Proven UI/UX design experience with a strong portfolio showcasing your work.
Proficiency in front-end development web programming languages such as HTML5, CSS3, JavaScript, and jQuery.
Experience with design tools such as Adobe Creative Suite, Sketch, Figma, or similar.
Knowledge of responsive design principles and techniques.
Experience in usability testing and user research methodologies.
Strong problem-solving skills and attention to detail.
Excellent communication skills and ability to collaborate with team members.
Understanding of cross-browser compatibility issues and ways to work around them.
Federal Experience is a plus.
