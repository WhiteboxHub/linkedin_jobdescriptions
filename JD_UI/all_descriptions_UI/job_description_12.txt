About the job
We are seeking a creative and experienced DTC UX/UI Designer to join our team. The ideal candidate will be passionate about designing intuitive, engaging, and seamless user experiences for our customers. You will work closely with cross-functional teams to bring our brand vision to life, optimize our e-commerce platform, and ensure a consistent and enjoyable user journey.
 Key Responsibilities:
  Conduct user research to understand customer needs, behaviors, and pain points specific to the DTC model.
Develop and maintain information architecture, wireframes, and prototypes to support design decisions.
Design visually appealing and user-friendly interfaces for our website and mobile applications.
Collaborate with marketing, product, and development teams to ensure design consistency and alignment with brand guidelines.
Optimize the customer journey from product discovery to post-purchase engagement, with a focus on conversion rate optimization (CRO).
Conduct usability testing and gather feedback to iterate and improve design solutions.
Ensure responsive design and functionality across various devices and platforms.
Create and maintain design documentation, including style guides and design specifications.

 Qualifications:
  Bachelor's degree in Design, Human-Computer Interaction, or related field, or equivalent work experience.
3+ years of experience in UX/UI design, preferably with a focus on e-commerce or DTC brands.
Proficient in design tools such as Sketch, Adobe XD, Figma, or similar.
Strong portfolio showcasing a range of UX/UI design projects, with a focus on DTC or e-commerce.
Excellent understanding of user-centered design principles and best practices.
Experience with usability testing and user research methodologies.
Strong visual design skills and attention to detail.
Ability to work collaboratively in a fast-paced, agile environment.
Strong communication and presentation skills.

 Benefits:
  Competitive salary and benefits package.
Opportunity to work with a dynamic and innovative team.
Flexible working hours and remote work options.
Professional development and growth opportunities.
A collaborative and inclusive work environment.
