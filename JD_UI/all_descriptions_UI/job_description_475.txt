About the job
Full-Time Direct Hire Opportunity with our Direct Client. 

About Our Client:  Join a dynamic and innovative team that thrives on creativity and collaboration. We work with one of the top agency clients in the Greater Milwaukee area, known for its fun and inclusive atmosphere. Here, your voice is not only heard but valued, and you'll feel like a true part of the team. We are seeking a talented Front-End Software Engineer who is passionate about building intuitive and responsive web applications.

Position Overview: As a Front-End Software Engineer, you will be responsible for developing and maintaining the front-end components of our client’s web applications. You'll work closely with their creative and development teams to deliver high-quality, user-friendly interfaces. If you have a strong foundation in HTML, CSS, Vue.js or React.js, and experience with Node.js/PHP, we’d love to hear from you!

Key Responsibilities:
Develop and maintain responsive, user-friendly web applications using HTML, CSS, and JavaScript frameworks such as Vue.js or React.js.
Collaborate with UI/UX designers to transform design mockups into interactive web experiences.
Work alongside back-end developers to integrate front-end components with server-side logic using Node.js.
Optimize web applications for maximum speed and scalability.
Ensure the technical feasibility of UI/UX designs.
Participate in code reviews to maintain code quality and best practices.
Troubleshoot and debug issues to improve application performance.
Stay updated with emerging trends and technologies in front-end development.
